Your Mental Wellness Check-In 🧠

Answer honestly. There are no wrong answers—just paths to support.

  • Do you often feel sad or empty?
  • Do you find it hard to enjoy things you used to love?
  • Do you struggle with anxiety or racing thoughts?
  • Do you feel overwhelmed even by small tasks?
  • Have you been sleeping too little—or too much?

If any of these sound familiar, you're not alone. Let's explore ways to cope together.

Mental Health Toolkit 🧰

  • 🌬️ Breathing Exercise: Inhale for 4, hold for 4, exhale for 6. Repeat 5 times.
  • 🎧 Guided Meditation: Try this calming video.
  • 📞 Crisis Line: If you're in the U.S. and need urgent help, call or text 988.
  • 🛌 Night Routine: Turn off screens 30 minutes before bed, stretch, and write a gratitude note.

Feeling Overwhelmed or Unsafe?

Your feelings are valid, and help is available. If you're having thoughts of self-harm or suicide, please reach out to someone.

  • 📞 Call or text 988 in the U.S. (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)
  • 💬 Talk to a trusted adult, mentor, or counselor
  • 🧘 Try grounding: name 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste
  • 💌 Write your feelings down—sometimes letting it out helps
